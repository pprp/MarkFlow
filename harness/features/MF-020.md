# MF-020 - Outline panel shows heading hierarchy and supports quick navigation in long documents

## Notes
Implemented with parser-backed heading derivation in `packages/editor/src/editor/outline.ts`, a right-hand outline panel in `packages/editor/src/App.tsx`, and navigation/highlight coverage in `src/editor/__tests__/outline.test.ts` plus `src/__tests__/App.test.tsx`, including duplicate-anchor handling, fenced-code exclusion, and live rename/reorder sync. Sources: https://support.typora.io/Outline/ and https://support.typora.io/Quick-Start/.

## Steps
- Open a document with nested headings from multiple levels.
- Show the outline panel and confirm it mirrors the heading hierarchy.
- Click outline entries and verify the editor scrolls to the target section and highlights the active heading.

## Automated Verification
- pnpm --filter @markflow/editor test:run -- src/editor/__tests__/outline.test.ts src/__tests__/App.test.tsx

## Manual Verification
- Optional interactive pass: open a long document and confirm the panel scroll feel remains comfortable after repeated heading edits.
