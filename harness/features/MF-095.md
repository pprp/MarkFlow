# MF-095 - Document statistics panel surfaces words, characters, reading time, and per-selection counts

## Notes
Typora exposes an expanded word count / statistics popover. Source: https://support.typora.io/Shortcut-Keys/.

## Steps
- Open the statistics panel (menu or status-bar click) and confirm live totals for words, characters (with/without spaces), paragraphs, and approximate reading time.
- Make a multi-block selection and confirm the panel adds a selection column alongside the document totals.
- Confirm counts ignore fenced code content when the 'exclude code' preference is on.

## Automated Verification
- Extend `wordCount.test.ts` to cover paragraphs, reading-time approximation, and selection/exclusion modes.

## Manual Verification
- Cross-check counts against Typora on a shared sample document.
