# MF-059 - Undo history is capped at 500 steps and compressed for large-file sessions to stay under 256 MB

## Notes
Implemented on 2026-04-12 in `packages/editor/src/editor/MarkFlowEditor.tsx` and `packages/editor/src/editor/historyLimit.ts`. The editor now configures CodeMirror history with a 500-event target and prunes serialized `historyField` state back to the newest 500 change events whenever the native branch grows past the cap, which keeps undo depth exact even though CodeMirror's built-in history only exposes a retention floor (`minDepth`) instead of a true max depth. Automated coverage in `packages/editor/src/editor/__tests__/MarkFlowEditor.test.tsx` proves that 600 isolated edits settle at exactly 500 undoable events and that the 501st undo stops cleanly; it passed on 2026-04-12 with `pnpm --filter @markflow/editor test:run -- --grep "caps undo history"`, plus `pnpm --filter @markflow/editor build` and `pnpm --filter @markflow/editor lint`. `passes` remains false because the manual heap-size verification for large-file sessions is still pending.

## Steps
- Make 600 distinct edits in a large file.
- Confirm undo walks back 500 steps and stops cleanly.
- Confirm heap size does not exceed 256 MB after 600 edits.
