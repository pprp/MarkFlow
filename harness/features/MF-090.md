# MF-090 - Open Recent tracks recent files and folders, with clear-history and pinned-location behavior

## Notes
Implemented with a persisted recent-history model in `packages/desktop/src/main/fileManager.ts`, dynamic `File -> Open Recent` menu generation in `packages/desktop/src/main/menu.ts`, and renderer handling in `packages/editor/src/App.tsx` so recent files and folders now survive restarts, feed Quick Open and OS recent-document sync, support pinned folders plus `Clear Items` / `Clear Recent and Pinned Folders / Files`, and report missing recent paths gracefully. Automated coverage in `packages/desktop/src/main/fileManager.test.ts` and `packages/desktop/src/main/menu.test.ts` passed on 2026-04-16, alongside `pnpm lint`, `pnpm test`, `pnpm build`, and `pnpm harness:verify`. `passes` remains false until `Open Recent`, Quick Open, and OS-native recents are manually verified after restarting the app. Source: https://support.typora.io/File-Management/.

## Steps
- Open several files and at least one folder across multiple sessions and confirm `File` -> `Open Recent` lists them most-recent-first.
- Pin a recent folder/location, clear recent items, and confirm pinned entries follow Typora's clear-options behavior while unpinned history is removed.
- Click an entry whose file or folder is missing and confirm MarkFlow reports the error gracefully instead of crashing.
