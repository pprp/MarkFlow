# MF-036 - Heading sections can be folded and unfolded to hide document subtrees while editing

## Notes
CodeMirror 6 ships `@codemirror/language` fold support via `foldEffect`, `unfoldEffect`, and `foldGutter`. Defining heading-aware fold ranges (fold from heading line to the line before the next equal-or-higher heading) and wiring them to the gutter gives long-document writers a powerful navigation shortcut with minimal implementation risk.

## Steps
- Open a long document with multiple heading levels and observe fold indicators in the left gutter.
- Click a fold indicator next to an H2 and confirm all content up to the next same-level heading collapses to a single summary line.
- Unfold the section and confirm the full content is restored without losing any characters.

## Automated Verification
- Add CodeMirror fold-range tests verifying that heading fold ranges cover the correct line spans and that fold/unfold state-cycles produce identical document content.

## Manual Verification
- Fold and unfold nested headings on a real document; confirm fold state persists across WYSIWYG↔source mode toggles.
