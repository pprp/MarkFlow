MarkFlow — Logo Round 2

Abstract marks, no letterforms. Each one commits to a single concept rooted in the product (language flow, text→render transformation, writer↔model graph) rather than generic AI sparkle.

Concept A

Aurora Stroke

One continuous tapered brushstroke with an S-curve and a detached leading spark. The asymmetry is intentional — it reads as a gesture, not a glyph. Warm amber→copper→olive keeps the mark editorial instead of generic AI.

Single gesture Warm gradient Asymmetric

Concept B

Refraction Prism

A rotated square split into four glass facets, each with its own warm gradient and a subtle catch-light. Prism = markdown refracted into rendered output. Glassmorphism is the dominant 2026 tech-logo aesthetic.

Crystal / facets Warm palette Product metaphor

Concept C

Flow Constellation

Three nodes on a continuous arc with one crossing chord. The glowing node is the AI — one of three points in a graph connecting writer, text, and model. Monochrome-first; single accent shines at dark/light toggle.

Minimal lines Monochrome-ready Graph metaphor
